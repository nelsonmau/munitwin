What MuniTwin does

Streetlights, roads, manholes, underground networks, green spaces — every asset mapped, monitored, and queryable. Know what needs fixing before it breaks.

Emergency Preparedness

Hydrogeological risk monitoring, hydrometric sensors, real-time alerts. When an event hits, your team has a shared operational picture — not a spreadsheet.

Extended module

Citizen Dialogue

Transparent, two-way communication between the administration and its community. Issue reporting, consultations, real-time updates on works in progress.

How data gets in

From raw territory to queryable twin

1

Upload existing records

Floor plans, CAD files, maintenance logs, cadastral data — whatever the municipality already has.

2

Aerial drone survey

A single drone flight captures the territory from above. No specialist contractor needed — a consumer drone is enough.

3

Street-level coverage

A car or bike with a camera covers the road network. Assets are identified and geolocated automatically.

4

Query & act

The AI structures everything into an interactive map. Ask questions in plain language. Get answers with GPS coordinates and priorities.
